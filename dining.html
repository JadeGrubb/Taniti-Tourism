Restaurants:

Taniti currently has 10 restaurants: five serve mostly local fish and rice, three serve American-style meals, and two serve Pan-Asian cuisine.

  • Local: The Fish Shack, The Rice Bowl, The Taniti Grill, The Beachside Cafe, and The Volcano View
  • American: The Burger Joint, The Steakhouse, and The Diner
  • Pan-Asian: The Sushi Bar and The Noodle House

Grocery Stores:

Taniti has two supermarkets, two smaller grocery stores, and one convenience store that is open 24 hours a day.

  • Supermarkets: The Market and The Grocery
  • Grocery Stores: The Food Store and The Fresh Market
  • Convenience Store: The Corner Store
