Regional Transportation:

  • Taniti features a small airport capable of accomodating small jets and propeller planes, with plans for expansion to accomodate larger jets in the next few years.
  • There is also a small harbor where a cruise ship docks one night per week.

Local Transportation:

  • Public buses serve Taniti City and run from 5 a.m. to 11 p.m. every day.
  • Private buses serve the rest of the island.
  • Taxis are available in Taniti City.
  • Rental cars can be rented from a local rental agency near the airport.
  • Bikes and helmets are available to rent from several vendors (helmets are required by law).

Taniti is very flat and walkable, with many tourists staying in the area surrounding Merriton Landing - which is easy to explore on foot.
